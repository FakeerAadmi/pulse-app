# Pulse Feature Roadmap

## Stage 1: Core Privacy & Friction Reduction (Immediate Polish)
*   **Remove "Clean Days" Streak Ring:** Replace with a "Clean Days this Month" percentage to eliminate the abstinence violation effect.
*   **Amoled Black Theme Option:** Add a true black (`#000000`) theme option to save battery and make graphs pop.
*   **App Lock & Stealth Mode:** Add FaceID/Fingerprint requirements to open the app, protecting user privacy from shoulder-surfers.
*   **"Nuke" Button:** A one-tap button deep in settings to securely erase all local data.
*   **Haptic Feedback Engine:** Add satisfying haptic "thuds" for logging intakes and a heartbeat haptic for the crave timer.
*   **Milestone Share Cards (Tier 1 Social):** Beautiful, client-side generated image cards for users to manually share to social apps.
*   **Commitment Contract (Tier 1 Social):** A locally stored personal statement surfaced during high-saturation moments.

## Stage 2: Biological Context & Predictability (Health Intelligence)
*   **Caffeine & Medication Interaction Tracker:** Add warnings for how nicotine metabolism affects caffeine (e.g., preventing withdrawal anxiety mistaken for caffeine jitters).
*   **Scrubbable Nicotine Curve:** Make the main graph interactive so users can drag their finger to see precise mg levels at specific times.
*   **Local Push Notifications:** Proactive, local-only pings when saturation hits 15%, warning of incoming physical withdrawal.
*   **Virtual Accountability Partner (Tier 1 Social):** Periodic local-only prompts asking "What would [Trusted Name] say right now?" during cravings.

## Stage 3: Advanced Ecosystem & Ecosystem Connectivity
*   **Home Screen Widgets & Live Activities:** Zero-friction logging from the iOS/Android home screen, and a Lock Screen Live Activity for the Crave Timer.
*   **Doctor / Therapist Export:** A PDF export specifically formatted for physicians/counselors showing daily mg averages, triggers, and sleep impact.
*   **End-to-End Encrypted Cloud Backups:** Mandate that if the user opts-in to cloud sync, the payload is client-side encrypted before transmission.
*   **Buddy System (Tier 2 Social):** Pair two users via a 6-digit PIN. End-to-end encrypted syncing of *only* streak and mode.
*   **Community Pulse Feed (Tier 2 Social):** Anonymized aggregate signal (e.g., "312 people in your city are tracking today").

## Stage 4: AI & Deep Behavioral Analytics
*   **Habit Coupling & Predictive Insights:** Active engine that notices patterns (e.g., "You log 90% of intakes with coffee") and proactively suggests environmental changes.
*   **Support Circles (Tier 3 Social):** Small, closed groups (max 5) sharing only streaks and daily emoji moods.
*   **Therapist / Coach Mode (Tier 3 Social):** Revocable, read-only web link showing a sanitized summary of trends.
